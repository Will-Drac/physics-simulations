Charge: - +

Mode:
Toggle Animated Equipotentials

Controls:

  • Click to draw charges surfaces with the selected charge
  • Press F to add a field line at mouse position
  • Press E to place or remove an emitter of particles with the selected charge at the mouse position
  • Press R to reload the page
  • Press 1 to set the selected charge to fully positive
  • Press 2 to set the selected charge to fully negative
  • Press 3 to go into erase mode
